Angus Chiang

            Angus Chiang was born in Los Angeles, and raised in Taiwan. He graduated from Shih Chien University, School of Fashion Design in 2013.

            In June 2013, Angus attended the contest of London Graduate Fashion Week and won the first place, International Show Award out from many competitors from different countries.

            Angus not only won the award, but also got the invitation from Fashion Scout to September 2013 London Fashion Week, The Graduate Showcase.

            In regards of creativity, Angus doesn't always have thoughts on designing clothes, but he can always find inspirations from his everyday life. Angus always likes to combine two different extreme different themes together to come up with all these different looks.

            Angus feels the blessed of having all the supports from the family. He is sure that with all the supports and his passion towards designing clothes, he will achieve his goals.

2013. Sailing To The Moon.

Using Taiwan’s traditional temple buildings, flags, and theater costumes as my inspiration,by combining Bauhaus ‘s Triadic Ballet formation. Imagine temple fair celebrations and people wish for Taiwan’s prosperity.We hope to use the same ideal by using astronauts as our medium,letting vibrated colors originating from temple fairs,navigating it into space, protecting the peace of all human beings.
